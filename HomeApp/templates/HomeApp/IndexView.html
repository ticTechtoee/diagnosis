Medical Imaging:

Our research in this area covers a wide range of topics, from development of imaging devices, image reconstruction, contrast media and kinetic modeling, image processing and analysis, and patient modeling. In addition to traditional clinical imaging modalities, such as CT, we are also interested in emerging technologies (e.g., functional and biological imaging techniques) for cancer detection. We are also working on four-dimensional imaging techniques so that the change of 3D patient anatomy with time can be visualized and analyzed.
